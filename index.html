OpenIPF Visualiser

Welcome to my openIPF visualiser!
To get started, download your file from the openIPF website. There should be a 'Download as CSV' button on your profile. Upload it here and select the filters that apply and bam.
Note: if you want to change the filters, you need to refresh/reupload the file.

THE GRAPHS ARE INTERACTIVE!!!
Hover over the points to see extra info or double-click on the lines in the key to look at a specific series of data!
